FAQ

Quick answers. If you don’t see your question, pop it in the enquiry form.

How far in advance should we book?

As early as you can — peak dates go quickly. If your date is soon, still enquire; we’ll tell you straight away what’s possible.

Do you handle dietary requirements?

Yes — we plan menus with dietary-led thinking from the start, so every guest feels included without it becoming a “separate menu” vibe.

Can we do cocktails or pairings?

Optional add-on. If you want a bar moment (welcome drink, signature serves, pairing notes), we’ll build it to match your story and schedule.

Where do you operate?

Edinburgh and surrounding areas as standard, with wider Scotland available depending on the event scale and logistics.
